Welcome to the Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facility (RVSF)

The Telangana's Innodatatics Vehicle Scrapping Portal is your trusted platform for responsible and eco-friendly vehicle scrapping. Our facility ensures that your old or damaged vehicles are scrapped in compliance with government regulations, promoting sustainability and safety.

Register your vehicle for scrapping, view registered vehicles, get quotations, and manage your account easily through our user-friendly portal. Join us in making Telangana cleaner and greener by scrapping vehicles responsibly.

🔧 Our Services at a Glance

At Vehicle Scrapping Facility, we provide a complete, end-to-end solution for responsibly scrapping old and unfit vehicles in compliance with Indian government norms. Backed by Innodatatics’ AI-powered backend, we ensure transparency, efficiency, and eco-friendly disposal from start to finish.

  • 🚗 1. Instant Vehicle Valuation (Get Quote)
    • Our smart quote engine calculates the real-time scrap value of your vehicle based on:
    • Vehicle make, model, and year.
    • Condition of the vehicle.
    • Current scrap market rates.
    • Simply enter your registration number or vehicle details, and get an instant estimate.
  • 📝 2. Documentation & RTO Deregistration Support
    • We handle all mandatory paperwork:
    • RC (Registration Certificate) validation.
    • RTO deregistration certificate.
    • Legal affidavit and scrapping certificate.
    • Our team ensures smooth compliance with scrapping policies under the Vehicle Scrappage Policy, 2021.
  • 🛻 3. Vehicle Pickup & Towing
    • We provide free pickup services from your doorstep across our Telangana coverage areas.
    • Vehicles are securely transported to our authorized scrapping yard using GPS-enabled towing trucks.
  • 🏭 4. Safe & Eco-Friendly Scrapping Process
    • Vehicles are dismantled at our authorized and pollution-controlled facility.
    • Hazardous fluids (oil, coolant, fuel) are safely drained and recycled.
    • Usable metals and components are processed with minimal environmental impact.
  • 📜 5. Certificate of Vehicle Destruction
    • Once scrapped, customers receive a Certificate of Destruction (COD) that:
    • Confirms lawful disposal of the vehicle.
    • Can be used to claim road tax refund and insurance cancellation.
  • 👥 6. Customer Support & Assistance
    • Our dedicated support team helps you:
    • Navigate the scrapping process.
    • Track the status of your vehicle.
    • Clarify doubts related to compliance and documentation.
  • 🌐 7. Digital-First Experience
    • The entire process—from quote generation to certificate issuance—is digital.
    • Users can track progress via their dashboard and receive updates by SMS/email.
